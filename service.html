Our Services

Name of service Description Images
Animal Rescue Our primary goal at Paws & Claws Haven is to provide animal rescue services.
Our mission is to rescue animals from mistreatment, exploitation, and euthanasia.
In response to emergency calls, our rescue crew works nonstop, offering prompt medical attention as well as secure transportation to our facilities.
After coming into our care, these animals get the food, medical care, and emotional support they require to recover.
Giving every rescued animal a second opportunity at a life free from pain and fear is our mission.
Pet Adoption We link loving families with animals in need of a forever home through our pet adoption program.
We meticulously evaluate every animal's requirements, disposition, and well-being to guarantee the most optimal fit with prospective adopters.
We offer direction and assistance to new pet owners to ensure a seamless transition into their duties through our comprehensive adoption procedure.
Adopting an animal not only results in a devoted friend, but it also has a significant positive influence by giving the animal a fresh start.
Veterinary Care Our first concern is the welfare of each and every animal, which is why we provide full-service veterinary care.
Our veterinarian team is prepared to manage all facets of animal health, from basic examinations and immunisations to urgent care and surgery.
Every animal in our care receives the necessary medical treatment from us to keep them happy, healthy, and prepared for adoption.
Our veterinarian services are available to the community as well, providing dogs in need with reasonably priced care.
Community Outreach Our Community Outreach initiatives are committed to promoting an ethical and compassionate animal-friendly society.
Through public awareness campaigns, educational workshops, and school programs, we hope to inspire people to take up the cause of animal care.
Additionally, we collaborate with neighbourhood groups to support immunisation campaigns, spay and neuter programs, and ethical pet ownership.
Our goal is to make the community more involved in order to make the environment safer and more compassionate for all animals.
